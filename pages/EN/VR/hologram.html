HOLOGRAM VR SHOW
(2020 PC - 2022 QUEST)

VR Software that let you play video files on scene, or in your own AR environment, to create a holographic concert show.

By using "Hologram Ready" videos, the software will use chroma key settings to create an hologram in your VR/AR environement.

We can also add background elements with other videos, or different visual effects.
