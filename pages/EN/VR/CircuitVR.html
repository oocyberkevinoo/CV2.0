Circuit VR (2021) - Meta Quest

A multiplayer game project in VR that use only one headset.

The player that put on the headset must draw a track.
The second player have to complete the track blindly, using audio clue from the player 1.

Each time the player is out of the track, his controller will vibrate to give a feedback of their wrong position, giving penalty to the score.

This game is not available publicly yet by lack of design, but is fully operational.



The game is compatible AR to create tracks directly in your own environement.
